About Elena

Elena Marchetti moved to Ashworth twelve years ago with a suitcase and her grandmother's sourdough starter. What began as weekend loaves shared with neighbours turned into a stall at the farmers' market, and then — three years ago — the little shop on Mill Lane that's become a morning ritual for half the town.

She mills some of her own flour using heritage grain from Carrow Farm, just eight miles away. Everything is baked on site, by hand, every morning before dawn. No shortcuts, no preservatives — just proper bread made properly.

"Elena isn't just a baker — she's the reason Mill Lane feels alive in the morning. The queue outside her door at 7am is as much about community as it is about sourdough."

What Meridian is about

A curated local directory that feels like a magazine — surfacing the real people behind your local economy. Not algorithms. Not ads. Just trust.

Built around people, not businesses

Meridian exists because generic directories don't serve communities well. They're cluttered, ad-driven, and full of anonymous reviews that erode trust rather than build it.

We took a different approach. Every person in this directory is here because they do great work and their community knows it. Being listed means something — it's curated, not paid for.

We believe the best way to discover who to trust is through real stories, real faces, and real recommendations from people who actually live here.

What makes us different

No ads, no pay-to-rank. Your position in the directory is never for sale. We don't run ads, we don't boost listings for money, and we never will.

No anonymous reviews. Instead of star ratings from strangers, we use editorial stories, community endorsements, and transparent summaries of public feedback.

No chains or corporates. This is for independent, local people only. If you're not rooted in the community, you won't find a place here.

🤝

Trust over traffic

We'd rather have 50 listings people genuinely trust than 500 nobody cares about. Curation is the whole point.

📖

Stories over stats

Every listing has a story behind it. We show the person, their craft, and their connection to the community — not just a phone number.

🏘️

Community pride

Being in the Meridian directory is something to be proud of. It means your community recognises the work you do.
