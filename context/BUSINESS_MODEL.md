# Business Model

## What It Is
A curated local directory that feels like a magazine — surfacing the real people behind a local economy.

## Core Value Proposition
- Trust-based discovery (not algorithm-driven)
- People-first listings (stories, not just contact details)
- Community pride as a feature — being listed means something

## What It Is NOT
- Not a generic listing site or search engine
- Not ad-driven or pay-to-rank
- Not for chains, corporates, or non-local entities
- Not review-dependent (no anonymous low-trust reviews)

## Target Market
- **Primary:** Local residents looking to support their community
- **Secondary:** People new to an area wanting trusted recommendations
- **Listers:** Independent local workers, makers, traders, service providers
