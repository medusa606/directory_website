# SEO Strategy

## Core Approach
Rank for **local intent + service/trade queries** that Google currently serves poorly with generic directories.

## Page Types & Target Patterns

| Page Type | URL Pattern | Target Query Example |
|---|---|---|
| Category page | `/[location]/[category]` | "plumbers in [town]" |
| Profile page | `/[location]/[person-name]` | "[Name] [trade] [town]" |
| Story/editorial | `/stories/[slug]` | "best [service] in [town]" |
| Location hub | `/[location]` | "local services [town]" |

## On-Page Essentials
- Unique title + meta description per listing
- Schema markup: `LocalBusiness`, `Person`, `Service`
- Location-specific H1s and content
- Internal linking between categories, profiles, and stories
- Fast load times, mobile-first, Core Web Vitals passing

## Content Strategy
- One editorial story per week minimum (indexable, shareable)
- Category landing pages with curated intro copy (not just a list)
- Noticeboard posts as fresh, crawlable content

## Link Building
- Local press/blog outreach when launching in a new area
- Cross-linking with listed people's own websites
- Community social sharing (organic, not paid)

## What We Don't Do
- No keyword stuffing or thin auto-generated pages
- No fake reviews or manufactured trust signals
- No cloaking or SEO tricks — trust is the brand
